WINE LABEL DESIGN

Concept Note

Building upon the neoclassical art style, the design reflects the period's emphasis on symmetry, proportion, and refinement. Neoclassical art was deeply inspired by the ideals of ancient Greece and Rome, characterized by clean lines, geometric precision, and a sense of harmony. These principles are embodied in the layout of the wine label, where every element is deliberately balanced to evoke a sense of order and sophistication.

The motifs surrounding Dionysus are reminiscent of the ornamental designs often found in classical architecture and frescoes. The intricate detailing draws inspiration from Greek and Roman friezes, incorporating subtle patterns that pay homage to the decorative arts of the era. By merging the visual language of neoclassical art with a contemporary aesthetic, this design serves as a bridge between history and modernity.
