ROOK & ROLL

Concept: The Rook and Roll logo combines the strategic essence of chess with a playful, gaming twist. A stylized rook is paired with a dice, representing the "roll" aspect of the name, symbolizing both strategy and chance. The dice adds an element of fun and unpredictability, while the rook anchors the design with its chess association. Bold, contemporary typography complements the visual, using a dynamic font to evoke excitement and engagement. The logo balances intellectual challenge with playful energy, making it perfect for a wide audience of both chess lovers and casual gamers.

MUSKAN

Concept:The logo for Muskan is designed to reflect her vibrant personality, incorporating bright, cheerful colors that exude positivity and energy. A smile is central to the design, symbolizing warmth, happiness, and approachability. The smile is subtly integrated within the logo, as part of the typography, creating a joyful and welcoming feel. A bold, lively color like red is used to evoke a sense of optimism and joy, perfectly capturing Muskan's essence. The overall design is modern, playful, and uplifting, making it memorable and full of character.
