BROCHURE DESIGN

This brochure was created to showcase the best food places in each part of Delhi, presenting information in a clean and engaging format.

Trifold brochure design | 4-day project | Individual project

SOFTWARES USED

Design Concept

The brochure design draws inspiration from the vibrant essence of Old Delhi, blending historic charm with modern aesthetics to celebrate the city's rich culinary heritage.

Exterior Design:The exterior cover features a textured brick wall pattern, evocative of the iconic red-brick architecture that lines the bustling streets of Old Delhi. This tactile design element not only reflects the historic character of the area but also sets the tone for a journey through Delhi's culinary and cultural landscapes. The use of deep, earthy tones and intricate detailing on the cover creates a sense of nostalgia, inviting readers to explore further.
Interior Layout Upon opening, the brochure transforms into an interactive map, providing a visual journey through Delhi's most celebrated food destinations. The map is designed with a harmonious balance of functionality and artistry:
Illustrations: Digitally crafted, vibrant icons and motifs, including plates of food, cutlery, and spice jars, represent different culinary hotspots, adding personality to each location.
Color Palette: A warm, inviting palette of reds, yellows, and greens complements the theme, mirroring the colors of spices and traditional Indian dishes.
Typography: Elegant yet legible fonts guide readers seamlessly through the map, ensuring clarity without compromising aesthetic appeal.

The design seeks to evoke a sense of discovery, encouraging readers to not only explore the food destinations but also immerse themselves in the cultural tapestry of Delhi. In essence, this brochure is not just a guide—it’s a love letter to Delhi’s culinary legacy, crafted to ignite curiosity and inspire exploration.
